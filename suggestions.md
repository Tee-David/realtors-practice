I want to augment the search experience for the search page in my app that feels alive and interactive, modeled after the high-end feel of Airbnb and Zillow.
When a user enters a query—like '3 bedroom flat under 5m in Lekki'—I want the map to trigger a smooth .flyTo() transition that glides the camera directly to Lekki. To make the discovery feel dynamic, I want to simulate a 'live crawl' effect: as the app fetches data from my database, I want the markers to stagger and 'pop' onto the map one by one using Framer Motion for a satisfying scale-up animation.
Instead of standard pins, I’ll use Custom DivIcons to display Price Pills (e.g., '₦5m') directly on the map so users see the value immediately. To pull it all together, I’ll sync the map with my results list—so hovering over a property card in the sidebar makes its corresponding map marker scale up or change color in real-time, creating a fully integrated, premium search experience."
